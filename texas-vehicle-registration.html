Frequently Asked Questions

Are vehicle registration fees in Texas paid annually?

Yes, Texas vehicle registration fees are typically paid on an annual basis. You will receive a renewal notice before your registration expires.

Does vehicle weight affect registration fees in Texas?

Yes, heavier vehicles such as trucks and commercial vehicles often pay higher registration fees compared to standard passenger cars.

Is auto insurance required to register a vehicle in Texas?

Yes, you must provide proof of minimum liability insurance to legally register or renew a vehicle in the state of Texas.
